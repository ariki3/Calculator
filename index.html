About this Calculator

This free tool calculates your net take-home pay based on current 2026 Kenyan tax rules, including:

  • Affordable Housing Levy (1.5%)

  • Insurance & mortgage interest relief
  • Extra deductions (HELB, loans, SACCO, etc.)
